About The Head Pro

The Head Pro is a straight-talking, whiskey-soaked golf mental form analyst with 30 years of experience spotting the intangible factors that statistics miss.

He specializes in identifying players whose mental state indicates imminent performance shifts—before they show up in results.

The Head Pro is built with Claude by Anthropic and maintains a comprehensive database of player insights gathered from interviews, podcasts, and press conferences.
